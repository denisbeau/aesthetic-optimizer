## üéØ KEY FINDINGS FROM PROMPT 1

### Top 6 Addictive Mechanisms (Strongest Evidence)
1. **Variable Ratio Reinforcement** - Intermittent rewards (AI ratings, tips)
2. **Zeigarnik Effect** - Incomplete tasks drive return visits
3. **Loss Aversion** - Streak systems prevent abandonment
4. **Tribal Rewards** - Social validation and status
5. **Endowment Effect** - Personalization creates attachment
6. **Flow State** - Balanced challenge/skill creates immersion

### Top 5 Feature Recommendations (Legal + High Engagement)
1. **AI Facial Landmark Reveal** - Variable reward, high session frequency
2. **Protagonist's Roadmap** - Zeigarnik effect, 30-day retention boost
3. **Jawline Consistency Streaks** - Loss aversion, high stickiness
4. **Aesthetic Potential AI Visualizer** - Endowment effect, high investment
5. **Archetype-Based Accountability Circles** - Tribal rewards, 90-day retention

### Critical Legal Requirements (Canada)
- ‚úÖ **Biometric Data:** Express consent required, local-first storage recommended
- ‚úÖ **Dark Patterns:** Illegal - no nagging, neutral design hierarchy
- ‚úÖ **False Scarcity:** Only legal if literally true (real countdowns)
- ‚úÖ **Minors:** High-default privacy, parental consent for under-13
- ‚úÖ **Quebec Law 25:** PIA required, CAI notification 60 days prior
